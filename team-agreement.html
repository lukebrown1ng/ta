Daily Sync

Each team member is asked to discuss their cards and ask themselves these 3 questions:

  • What cards have they moved?
  • Any blockers/bottlenecks?
  • What are you working on today?

Clarity

As a team we read the entire ticket, not just the subject. If the ticket is not crystal clear, we push back and do not start until we have clarity. Update the ticket with the outstanding questions that require answering and any confirmation on assumptions.

Team Board

It is everyone's responsibility to ensure the Jira board is updated with all ticket comments and updates before the daily sync.

Leave

It is your responsibility when taking extended leave to:

  • Update the team leave calendar
  • Handover any work, with updates on all tickets
  • Let the team know before you're on leave


Team

The team comes first. We respect our team members and acknowledge and respond asap, even if we don't have the full answers. If we need focus time we update our slack status and let the team know.

Slack

We use threads where possible to keep discussions together. We use emoji reactions to provide quick, timely feedback and acknowledgements. We mute / leave all channels that distract from our roles.

Coffee Breaks

As a distributed team we can't always have a normal coffee. We have virtual coffee breaks to help build team relationships.

Celebrate

We celebrate both wins and failures. These are moments we can celebrate success and learn from failure.


Pair/Swarming

We open zoom calls in our team channels to help pair and swarm on work. This helps increase knowledge sharing and transfer within the team, while creating visibility. We use synchronous comms where it makes logical sense - but we always update the team in the slack channels on the results and output.

Questions

We don't make judgements when people ask questions. WE embrace the fact we want to learn together and appreciate openness within the team. When we don't know something, we ask the team first then research second.

Feedback

We want to build a culture of giving and receiving feedback. We give feedback to people in a way they wish to receive it, but most importantly we give it on time an a factual manor.

Show & Tells

Building better visibility and sharing our knowledge, we demo our work to the team. Showcasing the great work we have done, but transferring knowledge to the wider team.


Meetings

Clear agenda, objective & outcome. Always communicate output from meetings into #ce-ccoe-team-annoucements.

Confluence

We all commit to keeping confluence updated and accurate as part of each peice of work we complete. We make the time to do this as part of the work we undertake.

Guides

Regardless how small or large we think a how to guid esould be, we should have one. Weather the guide is for our team, or for people outside the team, we make create and maintain them.

Decisions

We take ownership of ideas as a team. We understand why and what we are doing work for. Decisions should be documented in tickets, reference documents and decisision registers.
